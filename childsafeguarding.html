Child Safeguarding


At Blessington Educate Together N.S. we are committed to safe practice and appropriate responses to concerns about the safety and welfare of children. Child safeguarding is about protecting children from harm, promoting their welfare, and in doing so, creating an environment which enables children to grow, develop and achieve their full potential.


Each school must have a Designated Liaison Person (DLP) in place for dealing with child protection concerns.

The name of the Designated Liaison Person (DLP) at Blessington Educate Together N.S. is Anita Collings (Principal). The Deputy Designated Liaison Person (DDLP) is Lynn Steed.
